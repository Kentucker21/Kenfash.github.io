About Us

Humble beginnings

Kenfash is an online eccommerce platform that buys retail products and resells them at a reasonable price founded by the current C.E.O Keneil Watson,but how did this all start? For that we would have to go back to 2011, when Kenfash went by the name 'Ken's retail' running out of a small garage thrifting used products until we eventually went mainstream when we started shipping abroad to selected locations,made an online shopping website and rebranded to selling retail items from different distributors and renamed the company to KenFash

Rise to Prominence

After about a year of selling online our business started to gain traction to the likes of shein and temu and major clothing and footwear brands began to take notice and we signed partnership contracts with many of them such as the ones listed below:


We began buying storage warehouses and hiring packaging employees and before we knew it we had become one of the biggest clothing businesses in the country even making it to the new york times.

Company Goals

From the inception of this company our goal has always been one thing, to provide the best quality products at an affordable price for people of all walks of life and that has not changed. we wish to expand our team and our reach to more countries no matter how far. Just as the Ceo of our company said 'In order to grow we must first sow'.Do you want to grow with us and make this dream a reality?
